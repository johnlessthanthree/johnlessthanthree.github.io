CONCEPT:
The second and final album of the fear of death and the end of time:
This LP was written in a time of my life that was riddled with pure existential fear. Shortly after work began on this record, two loved ones of mine passed away; my dog and my grandfather both died within weeks of each other in October 2025. In a time where I was already contemplating the terrifying fact that I will cease to exist one day, these deaths both came at a very eerie time in my life. My grandfather's death in particular had a profound impact on me; his death was like a mirror to my own. This album thus mirrors the life of my grandfather as well as my grandmother (whom passed away a year prior in 2024) in a semi fictional concept. This record is a cautious expression on the inevitability we all face.
