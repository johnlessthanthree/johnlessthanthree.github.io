poetry
 

our destiny is to fade away:
We ignored the inevitable and embraced denial
for as long as our minds would let us.
But now that inevitable is right in front of us.
Though we knew we couldn't out run it,
we still ran anyways.
But now we're at the edge of this so called cliff named existence.
All we can do is look back
on the countless oblivious days we spent
unaware and blissfully happy,
unaware of the unpreventable brutalities
that would eventually engulf us.

from 'together we fade away'
