REVIEW:
This album gives me feelings I can't quite express, but I'll try. At times, it's almost meditative,but then comes in with sounds that evoke a feeling of yearning or nostalgia, lending itself to an overall journey of an experience that I'm very glad to have found. Hit me harder than I was expecting, but in the best way possible. 
~ allatonce-notnot-toofast on bandcamp

CONCEPT:
March 21st, 2025 I attempted to end my life. I shoved a plastic bag over my head, though I didn't fully expect to die, I just wanted to escape by any means necessary. After failing, I took the plastic bag off my head and went outside. While outside, I had a wave of clarity hit me and I discovered what there is to live for; a stark contrast to how I felt minutes prior. I desperately wanted death beyond anything else I had ever had desire for. Everything was all so abrupt and sudden. There was no steady build up towards my breaking point, a million things flooded me all at once and one thing led to another; the complete and total submission of my will to anything. This record is about that total submission. This record is about the impossible circumstances in which a human would surrender their entire existence. This is an expression of not only my fall, but also my spring; an expression of jubilee not only for myself, but for anybody else lost out there. This is an expression of hope in a world stained with pessimism.
