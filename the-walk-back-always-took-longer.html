CONCEPT:
The name is self descriptory. There is both no central concept and a central concept on this record. Each track embodies a different idea I'd have while on my walks, all in one conjumbled attempt to articulate the feelings I would have remincising on what short life I've lived thus far. 
A poem, because I really don't know how to describe this one:
Every once in a while I will walk just before sunset.
There's something about gazing at the ever fading stars
and the dystopian concrete boxes
that makes me feel a certain way.
I've found myself missing a lot of things
and a lot of people.
Nothing happens then everything happens
and then I'm sat wondering where and who everybody truly is.
But around the halfway point something always changes;
the bitter feeling of walking aimlessly
turns into a bittersweet feeling.
I find myself loving what I could not previously love.
It is in this occurrence I am always reminded;
the world you don't see is the world you won't see,
but it isn't the world you can't see.
