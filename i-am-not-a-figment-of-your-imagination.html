CONCEPT:
A piece of art written in gross regurgitation to the crimes happening at capitol hill; a piece of art written in purposeful disconformity. Seeing crimes of unimaginable horror happening in multiple places across the globe multiple times over has been nothing short of appalling beyond measure and beyond words. As art is my main avenue of of expression, it was only natural I wrote about this. I felt it wouldn't be appropriate to try and write within the standards of any major convention with this record... Though concepts of nonconformity alone can convey a great deal, applying that philosophy it to the actual sounds simply can do so much more. Most people likely wouldn't enjoy this record for the aforementioned reasons, and that's okay, that's intentional. The most potent statements are the ones that stand out.
